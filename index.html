About me

I am a computational biologist with the Single Cell Analysis Innovation Lab at the Sloan Kettering Institute, led by Dr. Dana Pe'er. I also collaborate frequently with Tom Nook in real estate endeavors, where I have applied my experience in biology to uncover a full fossil collection that has contributed to the meteoric rise of our island to a five star rating. When not studying living things, I can often be found consuming them. I am a connoisseur of chocolate, boba, cookies, and other cultivated foods.
